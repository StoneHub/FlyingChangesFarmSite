Where aviation meets farm life

Flying Changes Farm sits on both sides of Pumpkintown Road in the Blue Ridge foothills of South Carolina. For generations, our family has looked to the skies—and now we share this peaceful place with visitors, campers, and the animals who call it home.

A Flying Family

For generations, our family has called these foothills home—and we've always looked up. The private airstrip runs right through our pastureland, where Pinnacle Paramotor pilots train and take off into Carolina blue skies. The hangar serves as gathering space, workshop, and shelter when storms roll through.

Farm Life & Animals

Our horses and goats set the rhythm of each day. Rotational grazing keeps the hillsides healthy, and the animals are as much a part of the experience here as the views. Campers love waking up to the sight of them grazing in morning light.

What Makes Us Special

This isn't just a campsite—it's a working farm with a private airstrip. Unplug and experience Blue Ridge foothill living. Whether you're here for a quiet weekend or to watch paramotors take flight, you'll find peace here.
