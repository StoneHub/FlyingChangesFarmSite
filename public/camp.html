The Campsite

Our primitive campsites are located along the pasture fence line, offering wide-open views of the farm and the foothills beyond. Each site includes a stone firepit and plenty of space to spread out.

Amenities

  • Restroom: Clean facilities located in the hangar.
  • Hangar Lounge: A covered space to relax, charge devices, or wait out a rainstorm.
  • Water: Potable water access near the barn.
  • Power: Shore power hookups available for RVs (inquire for details).

Good to Know

Arrival

Check-in is flexible. Just let us know when you plan to arrive so we can meet you at the gate.

Pets

Well-behaved dogs are welcome but must be leashed around the livestock.

Fires

Campfires are allowed in designated pits only, subject to local burn bans.

Quiet Hours

We're a quiet farm. Please respect our neighbors and animals by keeping noise down after 10 PM.
