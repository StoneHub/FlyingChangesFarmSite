Primitive stays on Pumpkintown Road.

Campers tuck into the pasture-side clearing at Flying Changes Farm in Marietta, South Carolina. The site is primitive by design, supported by the hangar’s bathroom, lounge, and storm shelter. Email while we finalize the public Hipcamp listing.

Your Campsite

  • Spacious clearing beside the pasture with sweeping foothill views
  • Fire ring perfect for evening stories and s'mores
  • Picnic table for meals and gathering
  • Level tent sites with soft grass underfoot

Hangar Amenities

  • Clean restroom facilities with running water
  • Covered lounge area—perfect shelter from rain
  • Power outlets for charging phones and devices
  • Kitchenette with sink for food prep and cleanup

RV-Friendly

  • RVs and camper vans welcome on our gravel pad
  • Shore power available at the hangar for hookup
  • Easy access from Pumpkintown Road
  • Reach out ahead for guidance on parking and setup

What to Bring

  • Tent, sleeping bag, and camp chairs
  • Drinking water and any food you'll need
  • Lanterns or headlamps for after dark
  • Weather-appropriate clothing and rain gear
  • Firewood (or we can provide—just ask!)
