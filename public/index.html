About the Farm

Flying Changes Farm sits on both sides of Pumpkintown Road in the Blue Ridge foothills of South Carolina. For generations, our family has looked to the skies—and now we share this peaceful place with visitors, campers, and the animals who call it home.

The private airstrip runs right through our pastureland, where Pinnacle Paramotor pilots train and take off into Carolina blue skies. The hangar serves as gathering space, workshop, and shelter when storms roll through.
