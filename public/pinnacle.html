The Runway

Our private grass strip is maintained year-round. It's perfect for STOL aircraft and paramotors. Please contact us for current conditions and permission before flying in.

The Hangar

More than just storage, the hangar is the social hub of the airfield. It features a comfortable lounge area, workshop space, and restrooms for pilots and campers.

Learn to Fly

We host Pinnacle Paramotor, offering world-class instruction for aspiring pilots. Whether you're looking for a discovery flight or full training, Brooke and the team have you covered.
